About Us

Care When You Need It, Compassion Every Time

Veterinary Out-Patient Clinic (VetOPC) provides affordable pet care for dogs and cats in Orange, CA. Your pet is more than just an animal — they're family.

We focus on out-patient services, meaning no overnight stays or long waits. Our streamlined approach helps reduce anxiety for both pets and their owners, while keeping costs affordable.

Our licensed veterinarians and trained support staff are deeply committed to providing high-quality, compassionate medical care. We provide upfront cost estimates with no surprise fees.
